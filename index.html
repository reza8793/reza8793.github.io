The Family Cycle

The plight of a father and the sorrow and the griefs of a mother is immesurable and non-countable. There is no such machine or device by which this fundamental human characteristic can be measured, compared or judged. In our society and culture father usually takes responsibility for the income and different socio-eco related outdoor activities though the present trend shows a deviation from the old customs. Now in the modern and digital age, a lot of mothers engage themselves out of the indoor wall of the environment which has reduced the strong footings, bondings and to some extent the control in the family. Nevertheless, the steering of the authority, guidance, policy, decision making, etc. is more or less designed and implemented by the head of the family, that is the father. Even then the contribution of a mother is treated/regarded much higher. Her presence of activity is felt right from the pre-birth of a child and stretches to the minor silly thing - to mend the shoes. The mother is the nucleus of a family around whom all the members of a family move around. The loving affection, concern, and care of the parents is universal, and the same feeling and emotion are also found among all the other living creatures of this universe. I think that parents are the best friends of their children (pre-marriage) and the spouse is the post-marriage best friend.A child is successful as long as he or she can maintain and balance the cordial relation between the trio.

The numbers of the friend list gradually increase along with the addition of each child. And this is a unfailing continual cyclic process since the dawn of the civilization. Though this traditional and conventional hypothesis is an old and outdated thought to lot many young and juvenile as the present trend of the virtual world has made them isolated and diverged.

SHYLOCK OF TWENTY FIRST CENTURY

We all must know the name of 'Shylock' the 'pound flesh' greedy character created by William Shakespeare in his ageless play "The Merchant of Venice." Below is a story where you will find the 'Shylock' of the modern age. My eldest daughter is now an MSN ( Master of Science, Nursing ) student at the University of California, San Fransisco, UCSF. Before this, she had completed a Bachelor of Arts in Public Health from UC Berkeley and Bachelor of Science, Nursing from the John Hopkins University. The reason for listing her accomplishments above goes to show her residence in various cities of USA in pursuit of her educational purposes namely: Los Angeles, Berkeley, Culver city, Baltimore, Glendora. But she never had the situation or experience like the one she is currently facing in San Francisco. She and her husband (an Electrical Engineer, who works at Google) rented a studio apartment room barely 230 sq ft (including the kitchen, bathroom, etc.) in the city of San Francisco because of the proximity of her School. Similar to most of the real state owners their landlord is also Jewish, and by nature, most Jewish people are always very tricky, rigid and cruel. Besides $2150 for house rent, they pay fixed $150 for the water plus the power bill monthly. There is no car parking facility. They keep their two cars in the street and maintaining the vehicle in the road one has to be very careful about the street cleaning and different time sequence constraint. HERE COMES THE CLIMAX OF THE CAPTION. The water bill was realized in a very tricky and in a sophisticated way. Initially, no money was taken for the use of water and time passed but all on a sudden after six months they sent a note in the mail that 900$ is due to them. To their utter surprise when they wanted to know how such a significant amount is pending for long six months! The reply was that it was their water bill which they ( Landlord )forgot to realize. My Son in law when wanted to see the water bill they said that its a joint connection and statements of all units came together. Well, "can we see the joint bill, please." They refused to show the bill. The water consumption for two persons can not cost 150$ per month in consideration of their stay at home, except the weekend most of the time they remain out of the house. It is clear that the house owner is exploiting the tenants. One friend of her pays $500 for car parking which is free in every other city. Now let us see the real picture of water consumption cost! My son owns a house in Norwalk city in the Los Angeles county comprising of four bedrooms, one living room, one jumbo size kitchen cum dining room, and two bathrooms. The house has yards both on the front and back. Besides the lawns on end and front, there is a small lawn on the walkway outside the home plus lots of pots and ground beds along with the boundary walls. All most every day the grass of gardens and the plants of flowers and fruits are watered with sprinklers, and hose pipe and often the cars are also washed beside the considerable use of water in the washing machine. Now let us see how much the water bill becomes in other cities. Oh my goodness!On an average 60$ per month!! In comparison to this, the water bill of my daughter should not be more than 15$ under any circumstance!!!
